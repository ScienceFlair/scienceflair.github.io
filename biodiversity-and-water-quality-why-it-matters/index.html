Q3: How does climate change affect water quality and biodiversity?
Climate change is exacerbating many of the challenges to water quality and biodiversity. Rising temperatures can lead to increased evaporation, reduced water flow, and altered precipitation patterns, all of which can affect the health of water systems. Warmer waters can also lower oxygen levels, making it more difficult for certain species to survive. Climate change can also alter the distribution of species, disrupting ecosystems and the services they provide.
