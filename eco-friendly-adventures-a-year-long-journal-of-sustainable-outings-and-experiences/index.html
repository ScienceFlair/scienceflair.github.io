Eco-Friendly Adventures: A year-long journal of sustainable outings and experiences


A new year brings new adventures, and as we embark on our journey through 2022, let’s step forward in a mindful, sustainable way. Join me as I share my experiences from a year-long exploration into eco-friendly adventures across the globe. From my encounters with wildlife in Costa Rica to cycling through the picturesque countryside of Tuscany, I’ll introduce you to a myriad of sustainable outings that will not only leave you feeling content but also make a positive impact on the environment.

January: Sustainable New Year in Costa Rica

As my eco-friendly adventures begin, I find myself on the shores of Costa Rica, diving into the world of eco-tourism. January marks the start of nesting and hatching season for sea turtles. To witness the beauty of these ancient creatures laying their eggs is a truly humbling experience, one that promotes conservation and respect for nature. I spend several days at an eco-lodge, which contributes to local community development, supports wildlife preservation, and aligns with my travel aspirations for sustainable living.

February: Lunar Lanterns in Sendai, Japan

The second month introduces fresh experiences as I head to Sendai, Japan. Sendai is home to the famous Jozenji Street, which is illuminated by countless beautiful lanterns for their annual Moon Festival. As darkness falls, the city comes alive with color as people celebrate this cultural event that has taken place for over 300 years. In addition to joining locals in their festivities, I immerse myself in the culinary delights, offering a diverse gastronomical experience that naturally favors local, organic, and seasonal ingredients.

March: Delicious Cycling through Tuscany, Italy

In March, the appeal of Tuscany pulls me towards its lush landscapes and verdant vineyards. I rent a bicycle and embark on a journey that not only showcases the region’s picturesque views, but also its incredible cuisine. Italy’s Slow Food movement perfectly embodies the essence of sustainable adventure, and my cycling trip takes me to various Slow Food Presidia. Over the course of two weeks, I enjoy delicious plates of fresh, seasonal produce and learn about pasta-making, olive oil production, and wine-tasting, all the while merging my quest for delectable meals with my desire to protect and promote traditional artisanal foods.

April: The Great Outdoors in the Swiss Alps

As spring brings hope and the promise of warmer temperatures, the Swiss Alps beckon me for a transformative getaway. This breath-taking landscape offers the perfect platform for eco-conscious adventures: hiking through the beauty of sustainability initiatives such as water revitalization projects, solar-powered installations, and the use of recycled materials for ski resort infrastructure. The Swiss rejoice in the beauty of nature and prioritize its preservation, allowing my passion for sustainable travel to further flourish.

May: A Sweat-Inducing Adventure in Costa Rica

The month of May sees me return to Costa Rica, this time for an adrenaline-fueled adventure. As I zip-line through lush rainforests and hike to hidden waterfalls, the sustainable principles of eco-tourism continue to be emphasized. The surrounding communities benefit from the revenue generated by these endeavors, further perpetuating the circle of care for the environment and those who inhabit it.

June: A River Cruise through Germany’s Fairytale Landscapes

As June arrives, I continue cruising towards Germany on a stunning scenic riverboat that distinctly uses a bio-diesel engine. Along the way, our group visits traditional villages that exemplify the rich history, culture, and cuisine of Germany’s Rhine region. From sampling the famous Riesling wine to exploring vineyards along the Moselle River, my Sustainable adventure leaves me with unforgettable memories.

July: An Irish Sojourn

Next up, I find myself traversing the Emerald Isle. Cycling through charming towns, I witness both the beautiful landscape and its self-sufficient spirit. Ireland’s eco-programs are thriving, allowing me to experience the allure of sustainable living through a mixture of inspiration and education. From the Cliffs of Moher to Dublin pubs serving local craft beers, the sights and sounds of Ireland entice me and enhance my journey.

August: Safari Style with Responsible Wildlife Tours in Africa

A heart-stopping experience in August leads me to Africa, where I embark on a responsible wildlife tour, aiming to preserve the endangered species that call this magnificent continent home. Witnessing a herd of elephants at sunset, or approaching a pride of lions in the golden light of morning, never fails to transfix me. The various reserves I visit inspire me to advocate for conservation and immerse myself in sustainable, eco-friendly tourism.

September: Diving into New Zealand’s Underwater World

September takes me diving in the picturesque coastal waters of New Zealand. A UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Poor Knights Islands are a nature lover’s dream, offering a diverse array of marine life, clear waters, and limestone formations. Eager to respect the delicate balance of these ecosystems, I engage in sustainable diving practices and educational programs that protect wildlife and preserve their habitat.

October: Pursuing Active Buddhism in Japan

My journey continues in the land of the rising sun, where the practice of environmentally-friendly Buddhism captures my attention. Although a refreshing change of pace from my previous adventures, it is an enriching experience that emphasizes respect for all life and the harmony between humans and nature. Japanese gardens, often found near temples, become a haven where my inner peace unite with the beauty of environmental conservation.

November: Sustainable Farming in Vermont, USA

Winding down my adventures in Vermont, I learn about sustainable farming practices cultivated by passionate locals at a verdant organic farm. Here, I gain practical knowledge about what it means to live a sustainable life – from cohabitating with nature to maintaining harmonious relationships with soil, crops, and livestock. As I exchange stories and recipes with fellow farmers, I find inspiration daily in their dedication, infused with respect for ecological balance and preservation.

December: Gearing up for My Next Eco-Friendly Quest

As 2022 concludes and the winter chill starts to set across the globe, I eagerly begin planning my next eco-friendly adventure. The joy in giving back to our planet through experiences and responsible travel festivities has only strengthened my resolve. Whether I’m on the Other side of the world or in my backyard, I look forward to the possibility of spreading the word about sustainable practices and celebrating the beauty and magnificence of our shared planet Earth.

The conclusion of my year-long sustainable explorations opens the gates to an endless world of eco-conscious travel opportunities, connecting the seemingly disparate experiences that have shaped my journey. As I reflect on the memories forged, the lessons learned, and the impressions left on the world, I stand in awe of our planet’s boundless potential to recover and thrive, thriving together with us.

Each eco-friendly outing brings us a step closer to safeguarding our planet, promoting conservation, and cultivating a sustainable future. It’s up to us to commit to responsible travel and to immerse ourselves guilt-free adventure – how will you embark on your Eco-Friendly journey?
