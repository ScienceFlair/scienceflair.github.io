The Science Behind Tissue Engineering

Tissue engineering is a multidisciplinary field that combines biology, engineering, and medicine to develop biological substitutes that can replace or repair damaged tissues. The goal of tissue engineering is to create living tissues that can function similarly to native tissues. This is achieved by using a combination of cells, biomaterials, and bioactive molecules to create a three-dimensional structure that can support tissue growth and regeneration.
