The Impact of Artificial Intelligence in Drug Repositioning


AI-powered drug repositioning involves the use of machine learning algorithms to identify potential drug candidates for new indications. These algorithms can analyze large amounts of data, including genomic and proteomic data, to identify patterns and relationships that may not be apparent to human researchers.

Identifying New Indications

AI-powered drug repositioning can identify new indications for existing drugs by analyzing the molecular mechanisms of the drug and its potential targets. This can be done by using machine learning algorithms to analyze the molecular structure of the drug and its potential targets, as well as the biological pathways involved in the disease.
