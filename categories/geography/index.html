The interactions between these plates lead to various geological phenomena that shape our planet’s surface. In this blog post, we will delve into the effects of plate tectonics and how mapping them out helps us understand Earth’s dynamic processes better. Understanding Plate Boundaries One of the key aspects of plate tectonics is the concept of plate boundaries, where plates interact with each other. There are three main types of plate boundaries: divergent, convergent, and transform boundaries. Divergent boundaries occur where plates move apart, leading to the formation of new crust through seafloor spreading. Convergent boundaries involve plates colliding, resulting in the formation of mountain ranges, deep-sea trenches, and volcanic arcs. Transform boundaries occur where plates slide past each other, causing earthquakes along faults. ...
