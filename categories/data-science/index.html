In this blog post, we will explore the concept of transfer learning, its applications across various domains, and the techniques used to implement it effectively. Instead of starting the learning process from scratch, transfer learning allows us to benefit from the knowledge learned by the pre-trained model, thereby reducing the need for vast amounts of labeled data and computational resources. This approach is particularly useful in scenarios where labeled data is scarce or when training a model from scratch is impractical due to time or resource constraints. ...
