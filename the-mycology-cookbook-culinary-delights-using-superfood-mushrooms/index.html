Turkey Tail Teriyaki

First, gain access to culinary nirvana with the Turkey Tail Teriyaki—a dish that honors the nutritional benefits of turkey tail mushrooms while tantalizing your taste buds. Here’s a simple, step-by-step guide to making a unique culinary experience in your own kitchen:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups turkey tail mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons mirin
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch mixed with 3 tablespoons water
  • 2 cups cooked brown rice
  • 2 cups mixed baby greens

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine soy sauce, sugar, mirin, garlic, and ginger and simmer for five minutes.
  2. Add the cornstarch mixture to thicken the sauce.
  3. Sauté the sliced turkey tail mushrooms in a separate pan until they develop a golden-brown color.
  4. Mix the mushrooms into the teriyaki sauce and cook for an additional minute.
  5. Serve the Turkey Tail Teriyaki over cooked brown rice and garnish with mixed baby greens.
