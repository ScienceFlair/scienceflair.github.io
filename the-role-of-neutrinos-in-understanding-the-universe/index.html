The Role of Neutrinos in Understanding the Universe


Deep within the heart of matter and energy, there exist particles so elusive and invisible to our human senses that they have been blissfully ignored for centuries. These elusive particles are a crucial component in unraveling the secrets of the universe, taking us on a journey that leads to a profound understanding of the cosmos. These enigmatic particles are none other than neutrinos, stripped down to their essence, neutrinos are a miracle in scientific research for their subtlety and usefulness in mapping our grand universe. It is on this endeavour that they stand as undersized sentinels wordlessly overseeing the border of the universe.
