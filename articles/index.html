From acquiring new skills to recovering from injury, neuroplasticity plays a pivotal role in shaping our behavior and cognitive abilities, offering profound insights into the dynamic interplay between the brain and behavior. Meanwhile, neurogenesis refers to the generation of new neurons, particularly in regions such as the hippocampus, which is associated with learning and memory.
