Stellar kinematics is a fascinating field of study that delves into the intricate motions of stars within galaxies. Understanding the Motions of Stars Radial Velocity One of the key parameters studied in stellar kinematics is radial velocity. This refers to the speed at which a star moves along our line of sight, either approaching or receding from Earth.
