Treatment planning software with adaptive capabilities enables personalized treatment adjustments, leading to optimized treatment delivery throughout the course of therapy. 5. Quality Assurance Tools Implementing robust quality assurance tools within treatment planning software is crucial to verify the accuracy and quality of treatment plans.
