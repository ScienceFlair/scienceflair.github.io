In this blog post, we will delve into the world of homomorphic encryption, exploring its benefits, applications, and potential impact on data security. Homomorphic encryption is a form of encryption that enables computation on ciphertexts, producing an encrypted result which, when decrypted, matches the result of the operations performed on the plaintext. This means that data can be processed and analyzed while still in its encrypted form, preserving its confidentiality.
