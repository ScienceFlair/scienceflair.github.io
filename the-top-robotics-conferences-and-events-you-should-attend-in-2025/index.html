The Top Robotics Conferences and Events You Should Attend in 2025


As robotics continues its rapid evolution, 2025 promises to be a landmark year full of groundbreaking innovations and transformative ideas. Whether you’re an engineer, researcher, entrepreneur, or enthusiast, staying up-to-date with the latest trends and networking with fellow professionals is essential. Attending leading robotics conferences offers unparalleled opportunities to learn from experts, discover cutting-edge technologies, and connect with like-minded individuals across diverse domains such as automation, artificial intelligence, manufacturing, healthcare, and beyond.

Here’s an in-depth guide to the must-attend robotics conferences and events in 2025 that will equip you with fresh insights and open doors to new possibilities.

1. International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA) 2025

When: May 2025
Where: To be announced (rotates annually)
Focus: Robotics research, automation technology, robotic systems, AI in robotics

Organized by the IEEE Robotics and Automation Society, ICRA remains one of the most prestigious global platforms for robotics professionals. The conference features keynotes by top scientists, presentation of the latest research papers, hands-on workshops, and exhibitions of frontier robotic innovations.

Why Attend?

  • Access peer-reviewed research before it hits journals
  • Hands-on demos from leading robotics companies
  • Opportunities to collaborate with fellow academics and practitioners
  • Tracks covering topics from humanoid robots to swarm robotics and robot perception

ICRA is perfect for researchers and technical professionals aiming to be at the forefront of robotics science and its real-world applications.

2. Robotics: Science and Systems Conference (RSS) 2025

When: July 2025
Where: To be announced
Focus: Experimental robotics, systems integration, robotic vision, multi-robot systems

RSS emphasizes experimental results and novel robotic systems that push beyond theoretical approaches. It’s a more niche gathering compared to ICRA, focusing explicitly on scientific advances validated by experimentation.

Why Attend?

  • Intimate setting encouraging in-depth technical dialogue
  • Cutting-edge presentations and workshops on topics like robot learning and navigation
  • Great arena for collaboration for academia and industry researchers alike

Engineers and roboticists who thrive on pioneering experiments and evolving practical systems will find RSS invaluable.

3. RoboBusiness 2025

When: September 2025
Where: Typically held in the U.S. (exact location TBA)
Focus: Commercial robotics, investment in robotics startups, industry trends, automation solutions

RoboBusiness bridges the gap between robotic innovation and business application. The event draws robotics entrepreneurs, corporate strategists, and investors under one roof.

Why Attend?

  • Gain insights on how robotics is transforming industries such as logistics, agriculture, healthcare, and manufacturing
  • Networking with venture capitalists and business leaders fueling robotics growth
  • Sessions on regulatory environment, intellectual property, and go-to-market strategies

If your goal is to scale a robotics startup or deploy robotic solutions commercially, RoboBusiness provides critical knowledge and connections.

4. European Robotics Forum (ERF) 2025

When: March 2025
Where: Location varies across Europe
Focus: European robotics research policy, collaboration, robotics ecosystems

ERF is Europe’s premiere robotics event focused on policy, research funding, and fostering collaboration among academia, industry, and government.

Why Attend?

  • Updates on European robotics funding programs like Horizon Europe
  • Discussions on ethical, societal, and regulatory challenges in robotics
  • Platforms to present joint research proposals or build cross-border alliances

For researchers and companies involved with the European robotics landscape, ERF is a must-attend for shaping future initiatives.

5. Automate 2025

When: Late April 2025
Where: Detroit, Michigan, USA
Focus: Automation, robotics solutions, artificial intelligence, machine vision, motion control

Automate is North America’s largest showcase of automation technologies, including robotic arms, AI software, machine learning capabilities, and sensors.

Why Attend?

  • Extensive trade show with over 400 exhibitors demonstrating the newest robotics applications
  • Educational sessions and keynote presentations from automation pioneers
  • Spot trends reshaping factory floors, warehouses, and fulfillment centers

Manufacturers and process engineers exploring automation integration will find immense value at Automate.

6. International Symposium on Robotics (ISR) 2025

When: June 2025
Where: Varies globally
Focus: Broad robotics disciplines, particularly industrial, medical, and service robotics

ISR is one of the oldest robotics conferences and maintains a balanced mix of scientific presentations, industrial showcases, and keynote addresses.

Why Attend?

  • Rich history and strong academic-industrial interface
  • Broad coverage of robotics sectors from surgical robots to autonomous vehicles
  • Excellent for those seeking interdisciplinary understanding or entry into new robotic fields

This event suits professionals aiming to broaden their robotics expertise across multiple domains.

7. RoboSense Summit 2025

When: November 2025
Where: Silicon Valley, California
Focus: Robotics sensing technologies, LIDAR, computer vision, sensor fusion

The RoboSense Summit focuses on sensory and perception systems that power modern robots’ intelligence and autonomy.

Why Attend?

  • Presentations from sensor manufacturers and AI vision experts
  • Hands-on workshops to test and develop sensing solutions
  • Deep dive into how perception enables applications like self-driving cars and drones

Engineers and developers working on robotic sensing and navigation technologies will find this summit highly relevant.

8. International Symposium on Wearable Robotics (WeRob) 2025

When: August 2025
Where: To be announced
Focus: Exoskeletons, prosthetics, wearable support devices, rehabilitation robotics

WeRob showcases innovations in wearable robotic systems aimed at human augmentation and medical applications.

Why Attend?

  • Latest research on design, control, and clinical trials of wearable robots
  • Interaction with medical professionals applying robotics in rehabilitation
  • Opportunities for collaboration between robotics engineers and healthcare specialists

Those involved in biomedical robotics and assistive technology should not miss this specialized symposium.

9. Consumer Robotics Expo 2025

When: October 2025
Where: Las Vegas, Nevada, USA
Focus: Consumer robotics, smart homes, personal robots, AI assistants

Consumer Robotics Expo highlights robotic products designed for day-to-day life — from cleaning robots and toys to security and personal assistants.

Why Attend?

  • Discover trends in consumer robot adoption and user experience design
  • Meet startups and major players targeting the home robotics market
  • Get a preview of future products shaping lifestyle and convenience

If you’re interested in how robotics intersects with everyday life, this expo offers a comprehensive view.

10. Asia-Pacific Robotics Summit 2025

When: December 2025
Where: Singapore or Tokyo (rotating)
Focus: Robotics development and deployment focused on the Asia-Pacific region

As Asia-Pacific leads much of the robotics manufacturing and integration worldwide, this regional summit provides insights into market trends, government initiatives, and innovation hubs.

Why Attend?

  • Access to booming markets in China, Japan, Korea, and Southeast Asia
  • Insights on labor automation in industries like electronics, automotive, and logistics
  • Connect with regional innovators, policymakers, and manufacturers

Attending this summit is strategic for companies or researchers aiming to strengthen ties or expand their footprint in Asia-Pacific.

How to Choose Which Robotics Events to Attend in 2025

Given the richness of robotics conferences, deciding which to attend depends on your priorities:

  • Research Focus: Conferences like ICRA, RSS, and ISR are ideal if you seek depth in scientific and technical innovation.
  • Commercial and Industry Networking: Events such as RoboBusiness, Automate, and Consumer Robotics Expo center around business growth and practical applications.
  • Regional Influence: If you operate primarily in Europe or Asia-Pacific, ERF and the Asia-Pacific Robotics Summit provide tailored content and stakeholder access.
  • Specialty Fields: For niche areas like wearable robotics or sensing technology, attending WeRob or RoboSense Summit lets you delve into specialized discussions.

Many events now offer hybrid attendance options, combining the benefits of virtual participation with in-person networking. Early registration often brings discounted rates and access to exclusive content.

Preparing to Get the Most Out of Robotics Conferences

Maximize your event experience with these strategies:

  • Research speakers and sessions: Identify workshops and panels most aligned with your interests in advance.
  • Plan your networking: Reach out to peers and schedule meetings to build meaningful connections.
  • Engage actively: Ask questions during sessions, visit exhibitor booths, and participate in social events.
  • Follow up: After the event, stay in touch with new contacts and apply the knowledge gained promptly.

Looking Ahead: Robotics Trends Highlighted in 2025 Conferences

Throughout the year’s events, several trends stand out:

  • Human-Robot Collaboration: Increasing focus on robots working safely alongside humans in factories and public spaces.
  • AI-Driven Autonomy: Advances in machine learning improving robot perception, decision-making, and adaptability.
  • Sustainable Robotics: Emphasis on energy-efficient designs and robotics supporting green tech applications.
  • Healthcare Robots: Progress in surgical robots, rehabilitation devices, and pandemic response robots.
  • Robot Ethics and Policy: Growing dialogue on responsible AI, robot rights, and regulation frameworks.

Engaging with these topics via conferences will help foresighted professionals anticipate and influence robotics’ trajectory.

Conclusion

Attending top robotics conferences in 2025 opens doors to innovation, collaboration, and business success in a field transforming every industry. Whether your interest lies in academic research, commercial deployment, emerging technologies, or regional dynamics, 2025’s events offer something vital for every robotics advocate. Mark your calendar, plan strategically, and immerse yourself in the exciting world of robotics to stay ahead of the curve.

By continuously connecting with frontline experts and witnessing the latest breakthroughs, you’ll be perfectly positioned to contribute meaningfully to the future of robotics. Your next big opportunity may well begin with a conversation at one of these remarkable events.
