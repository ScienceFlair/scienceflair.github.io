The Best Gardening YouTube Channels to Follow


Gardening has become a beloved hobby for many, whether you’re growing flowers, vegetables, or herbs in your backyard or managing an indoor garden. One of the easiest ways to learn new techniques, discover fresh ideas, and stay motivated in your gardening journey is by tuning into YouTube. The platform hosts a variety of gardening channels, each offering unique perspectives, tips, and insights for gardeners at any level. Whether you’re a seasoned green thumb or a beginner with a budding interest, there’s a channel for you.

In this post, we’ll explore some of the best gardening YouTube channels to follow, each offering something special that will help you grow your garden with confidence.

1. Garden Answer

Garden Answer is one of the most popular gardening channels on YouTube, with over a million subscribers. Hosted by Laura, this channel covers a wide range of topics, from container gardening to garden design ideas and seasonal planting guides.

What makes Garden Answer stand out is Laura’s approachable and friendly manner. She provides detailed tutorials on everything from choosing the right plants for your zone to creating stunning garden arrangements. If you’re someone who loves DIY projects, this channel will inspire you to transform your garden space with creative projects and beautiful decor.

Why Follow Garden Answer:

  • In-depth tutorials and plant care guides.
  • Seasonal tips for all types of gardens.
  • Stylish garden projects that add beauty to any space.

2. Epic Gardening

If you’re looking for a channel that blends practicality with expert advice, Epic Gardening is the one to check out. Founded by Kevin Espiritu, Epic Gardening focuses on simplifying gardening, making it accessible for anyone, regardless of space or experience.

Kevin’s content covers a variety of gardening styles, from hydroponics and aquaponics to container gardening and indoor plant care. He’s known for breaking down complex gardening concepts into easy-to-understand steps, making this channel perfect for beginners and advanced gardeners alike. Kevin also shares his own personal experiences with growing different plants, offering invaluable insights into what works and what doesn’t.

Why Follow Epic Gardening:

  • A wide range of gardening techniques, from hydroponics to permaculture.
  • Detailed, step-by-step explanations for each project.
  • Practical advice for both small and large-scale gardens.

3. The Horticult

For those who appreciate a more refined approach to gardening, The Horticult is an excellent choice. This channel, run by Summer Rayne Oakes, focuses on sustainable gardening, eco-friendly practices, and stylish plant care. Summer’s passion for plants and sustainability is evident in every video, making this channel not only educational but also inspiring.

From urban gardening to growing rare houseplants, Summer offers creative ways to incorporate greenery into your home and garden. Her tips are well-researched, and she often collaborates with experts in the field to provide deeper insights into the science of gardening.

Why Follow The Horticult:

  • Focus on sustainability and eco-friendly gardening practices.
  • Tips for growing a variety of plants, including rare species.
  • Elegant and stylish ways to bring plants into your living spaces.

4. Self-Sufficient Me

If you’re interested in learning how to grow your own food and become more self-sufficient, Self-Sufficient Me is a must-follow. Hosted by Mark, this channel is dedicated to growing food, raising animals, and achieving greater self-sufficiency in everyday life.

Mark shares his own journey of creating a self-sustaining lifestyle, offering advice on everything from building a vegetable garden to raising chickens and bees. The channel is particularly great for those looking to make their garden not just a hobby but a source of food and resources.

Why Follow Self-Sufficient Me:

  • Focus on sustainable living and food production.
  • Practical tips for growing food in small and large spaces.
  • Insights into raising animals and creating a self-sufficient homestead.

5. Laura LeBoutillier

For a more personal touch in gardening, Laura LeBoutillier offers an inviting and down-to-earth approach. As the creator behind the channel, Laura provides helpful gardening advice while maintaining a casual, conversational tone. Her videos are a great blend of practical advice and passion for gardening.

Her content covers a broad spectrum of gardening topics, from growing edible plants to designing flower beds. Laura’s detailed instructions are perfect for gardeners who want to learn how to make their garden spaces truly unique and beautiful.

Why Follow Laura LeBoutillier:

  • Engaging and friendly presentation style.
  • Tips for both beginners and experienced gardeners.
  • In-depth advice on growing a variety of plants, from flowers to vegetables.

6. Planterina

If you’re into houseplants and want to build your indoor garden, Planterina is an amazing channel to follow. Hosted by Amanda, this channel focuses on indoor gardening, plant care, and decor. Amanda’s expertise in houseplants shines through as she guides viewers through proper care routines for all kinds of plants, from succulents to tropical species.

What sets Planterina apart is its detailed approach to understanding plant health. Amanda offers insightful advice on how to troubleshoot common plant problems, such as pest infestations or improper watering, making it a valuable resource for indoor gardeners.

Why Follow Planterina:

  • Focuses on indoor gardening and houseplant care.
  • Provides troubleshooting tips for common plant problems.
  • Stylish tips for incorporating plants into your home decor.

7. BHG – Better Homes & Gardens

For more general gardening inspiration, Better Homes & Gardens offers a variety of videos covering not just gardening but also home improvement and lifestyle content. Their gardening section is full of tips on growing plants, landscaping ideas, and how to make your outdoor space more inviting.

Their team of gardening experts provides advice on everything from plant selection to garden design. If you’re looking for a mix of inspiration and practical advice, this channel will not disappoint.

Why Follow BHG – Better Homes & Gardens:

  • Broad range of gardening topics, from landscaping to plant care.
  • Stylish outdoor and indoor design ideas.
  • Expert advice on creating beautiful garden spaces.

8. The RHS – Royal Horticultural Society

If you’re someone who is serious about expanding your gardening knowledge, the Royal Horticultural Society (RHS) channel is a must-follow. The RHS is one of the most reputable gardening organizations in the world, and their YouTube channel is filled with expert-led tutorials, plant care advice, and garden tours.

What sets RHS apart is its comprehensive and scientific approach to gardening. Their videos dive deep into topics like plant biology, soil science, and pest management, making it an excellent resource for gardeners who want to elevate their expertise.

Why Follow RHS – Royal Horticultural Society:

  • Expert-led content from one of the world’s leading horticultural organizations.
  • In-depth videos on plant science, soil health, and more.
  • Educational and authoritative content for serious gardeners.

9. Mr. Plant Geek

If you love plants but want to add a bit of humor and personality to your gardening experience, Mr. Plant Geek is the channel for you. Hosted by Michael Perry, a plant enthusiast and expert, this channel brings a fun and entertaining twist to gardening content.

Michael’s personality shines through in every video, making plant care and gardening tips feel fun rather than formal. His channel covers everything from garden tours to plant care tips, and he’s always up for trying something new and unusual in the world of plants.

Why Follow Mr. Plant Geek:

  • Fun and entertaining gardening videos.
  • A mix of quirky plant ideas and expert tips.
  • Great for those who want to make gardening more enjoyable.

10. Growing Your Greens

For those passionate about growing food, Growing Your Greens offers tons of valuable content focused on organic gardening, food production, and self-sufficiency. Hosted by John Kohler, this channel dives into everything from composting and creating raised garden beds to growing food in small spaces.

What makes Growing Your Greens unique is its emphasis on growing food in an organic, sustainable manner. John’s energy and enthusiasm are contagious, making it easy for viewers to feel inspired to grow their own food and take control of their food sources.

Why Follow Growing Your Greens:

  • Focuses on organic gardening and food production.
  • Offers tips for gardening in small and urban spaces.
  • Inspires viewers to take a sustainable approach to food.

Conclusion

There’s no shortage of amazing gardening content on YouTube, and the channels we’ve covered here offer a wealth of knowledge, creativity, and inspiration for gardeners of all levels. Whether you’re interested in growing your own food, learning how to care for houseplants, or designing a beautiful outdoor space, the right YouTube channel can help you achieve your gardening goals.

Start following these channels today, and take your gardening skills to new heights!
