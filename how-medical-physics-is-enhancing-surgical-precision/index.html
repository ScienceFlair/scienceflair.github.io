Conclusion

Medical physics has undeniably transformed the field of surgery, making procedures more precise, less invasive, and safer for patients. The collaboration between physicists, surgeons, and other healthcare professionals is essential to harnessing the full potential of these advancements, ensuring that the future of surgery remains as safe and effective as possible.
