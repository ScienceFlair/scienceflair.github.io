The Best Astronomy YouTube Channels to Follow


Astronomy has always captivated the human imagination, from ancient civilizations gazing at the stars to modern scientists exploring the cosmos. With the rise of digital media, YouTube has become a treasure trove of astronomical content, offering enthusiasts and novices alike a chance to delve into the mysteries of the universe. Whether you’re interested in astrophysics, stargazing tips, or the latest discoveries in space exploration, there’s a channel for you. In this post, we’ll explore some of the best astronomy YouTube channels that you should follow to expand your knowledge and appreciation of the cosmos.

1. PBS Space Time

PBS Space Time is a channel that dives deep into the complexities of astrophysics and cosmology. Hosted by Matt O’Dowd, a professor of physics and astronomy, the channel presents complex topics in an engaging and accessible manner. Each episode explores fundamental questions about the universe, such as the nature of black holes, the fabric of spacetime, and the possibility of multiverses. The channel is known for its high production quality and thorough research, making it a must-follow for anyone serious about understanding the universe.

Why Follow PBS Space Time?

  • In-depth Analysis: The channel provides detailed explanations of complex theories.
  • Engaging Visuals: High-quality animations help illustrate difficult concepts.
  • Expert Insights: The host’s academic background lends credibility to the content.

2. Fraser Cain - Universe Today

Fraser Cain, the founder of Universe Today, brings a wealth of knowledge and enthusiasm to his YouTube channel. The content ranges from news about recent astronomical discoveries to educational videos explaining various celestial phenomena. Fraser’s approachable style makes complex topics digestible for viewers of all backgrounds. He often features interviews with scientists and experts, providing a well-rounded perspective on current events in astronomy.

Why Follow Fraser Cain?

  • Timely Updates: Stay informed about the latest discoveries and events in astronomy.
  • Expert Interviews: Gain insights from leading scientists in the field.
  • Diverse Topics: Explore a wide range of subjects, from planetary science to cosmology.

3. Scott Manley

Scott Manley is a popular figure in the astronomy community, known for his engaging and informative videos that often focus on space exploration and rocket science. With a background in astrophysics, Scott combines his passion for space with a knack for storytelling. His channel features everything from tutorials on how to use space simulation software to detailed analyses of space missions and rocket launches.

Why Follow Scott Manley?

  • Unique Perspective: Scott’s background in astrophysics provides a solid foundation for his content.
  • Entertaining Style: His engaging storytelling keeps viewers hooked.
  • Hands-On Tutorials: Learn how to explore space through simulation software.

5. The Bad Astronomer

Phil Plait, known as The Bad Astronomer, is a prominent figure in the science communication community. His channel focuses on debunking myths and misconceptions about astronomy and science in general. Phil’s engaging personality and humor make learning about astronomy fun and accessible. He covers a variety of topics, from the science behind popular movies to the latest discoveries in space.

Why Follow The Bad Astronomer?

  • Myth-Busting: Learn the truth behind common misconceptions in astronomy.
  • Humorous Approach: Phil’s wit makes complex topics enjoyable.
  • Wide Range of Topics: From science fiction to real-world astronomy, there’s something for everyone.

6. AstroBackyard

If you’re interested in astrophotography, AstroBackyard is the channel for you. Hosted by Trevor Jones, this channel focuses on capturing stunning images of celestial objects. Trevor shares tips and techniques for both beginners and experienced astrophotographers, covering everything from equipment reviews to post-processing techniques. His passion for the night sky is infectious, and his tutorials are easy to follow.

Why Follow AstroBackyard?

  • Astrophotography Tips: Learn how to capture breathtaking images of the night sky.
  • Equipment Reviews: Get insights on the best gear for astrophotography.
  • Community Engagement: Trevor often interacts with his audience, fostering a sense of community.
