Anatomy and Animal Welfare: Comparing Human and Non-Human Bodies

As we delve into the wondrous realm of anatomy and its profound implications for human health and well-being, it is equally essential to recognize the fascinating parallels and distinctions that exist between the anatomical compositions of humans and non-human animals.
