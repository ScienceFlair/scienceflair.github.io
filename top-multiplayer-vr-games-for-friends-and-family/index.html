Top Multiplayer VR Games for Friends and Family


Virtual reality (VR) has transformed the way we engage with entertainment, offering immersive experiences that go beyond the boundaries of traditional gaming. One of the most thrilling aspects of VR is the opportunity to connect with friends and family in shared virtual worlds, creating memories through teamwork, competition, and exploration. Multiplayer VR games have grown exponentially in popularity, offering diverse gameplay options that cater to every kind of player. Whether you’re looking for cooperation, friendly rivalry, or just a reason to laugh, these games are perfect for connecting through the magic of VR.

Why Multiplayer VR Games Are Perfect for Social Gatherings

The beauty of multiplayer VR games lies in their ability to bring people together in ways that feel truly interactive. Traditional video games allow for co-op and competition, but VR adds an entirely new layer by immersing players in the same virtual environment. This sense of presence, combined with intuitive motion-based controls, makes moments more meaningful. It’s not just about pressing buttons but actually moving, interacting, and engaging with other players as though you’re physically in the same room.

Whether you’re hosting a game night, spending quality time with family, or catching up with friends online, multiplayer VR games offer endless opportunities to create shared experiences. They’re also perfect for breaking the ice, providing fun challenges to encourage teamwork, and ensuring there’s no shortage of laughter.

Here’s a curated list of top multiplayer VR games designed to bring people together for hours of enjoyment. No matter your skill level or the type of game you love most, there’s something here for everyone.

1. Rec Room

Rec Room is a social VR platform that offers countless mini-games, entertainment options, and creative outlets. From laser tag to cooperative quests, there’s no shortage of activities to dive into. What makes Rec Room particularly appealing is its inclusivity—players can interact with others from around the world regardless of the headset they’re using.

For family-friendly fun, group up for a team adventure in one of the elaborate quest maps. If you’re playing with friends, you can explore user-generated games or even create your own. The creative tools are intuitive, and the possibilities are nearly limitless.

Social features like voice chat and customizable avatars add to the sense of connection. Whether you’re feeling competitive or simply want to hang out in an interactive hub, Rec Room ensures hours of fun tailored to your preferences.

2. Among Us VR

The hit social deduction game, Among Us, gets an exciting twist in its VR version. Perfect for friends and family looking for an adrenaline-pumping yet hilarious experience, Among Us VR drops you into a spaceship where teamwork—and betrayal—takes center stage.

Players take on roles as either Crewmates or Impostors, with Crewmates tasked with completing objectives while Impostors secretly sabotage and eliminate them. The VR adaptation makes meetings and accusations even more thrilling. Imagine trying to prove your innocence while someone else points a finger in your face virtually—everything feels so much more intense.

Since games are short and fast-paced, Among Us VR is ideal for larger groups and gatherings. Those who enjoyed the original will find the VR version even more immersive and fun.

3. Walkabout Mini Golf

Sometimes, simplicity is the key to a great multiplayer experience, and Walkabout Mini Golf excels in providing just that. This charming VR mini-golf simulator combines relaxing gameplay with enough challenge to keep everyone entertained. Choose from beautifully crafted courses set in stunning environments, and explore hidden secrets as you play.

The multiplayer mode lets you compete or just casually hang out with friends and family. It’s an ideal way to enjoy a laid-back session while still feeling fully immersed in its scenic locales. The game’s intuitive mechanics make it especially suited for players who are new to VR. Whether you’re aiming for a hole-in-one or laughing at missed shots, Walkabout Mini Golf is bound to become a favorite.

4. Pictionary VR (Aka Tilt Brush Multiplayer Concepts)

Pictionary-style games have always been a hit in home gatherings, and the VR adaptation brings even more creativity to the table. While there’s no official “Pictionary VR,” players can modify creative applications like Tilt Brush Multiplayer to enjoy this game. It requires participants to draw objects or scenes while others guess—VR elevates the game with 3D creation tools.

For family-friendly experiences, it’s an ideal way to put your artistic skills to the test. Children and adults alike can laugh and bond as they attempt to craft drawings in virtual space. This category of multiplayer creativity apps takes social gaming and creativity to new dimensions, making it perfect for gatherings.

5. The Climb 2

If your friends or family members enjoy thrilling experiences, The Climb 2 offers a multiplayer mode where players can compete to conquer massive cliffs and vertical challenges. Set across gorgeous terrain, the game lets players scale buildings, cliffs, and mountains while basking in the dazzling visuals of their surroundings.

In multiplayer mode, players can race against each other to reach the summit or compete for the fastest time. It’s an adrenaline-fueled activity that offers both challenge and exhilaration. If you have someone in the group who loves adventure or tests of endurance, The Climb 2 is sure to satisfy.

6. VRChat

VRChat is less a “game” and more a sandbox for social interaction. With thousands of custom worlds and experiences to explore, this platform lets you hang out with friends in whimsical or bizarre virtual environments that range from dance floors to amusement parks—and everything in between.

Customization is key here: avatars, interactive props, and roleplaying systems make VRChat endlessly entertaining. For an immersive multiplayer VR experience, join friends and family as you try escape rooms, karaoke, or just a relaxing movie night in VR. It’s the playground for anyone wanting to explore vast creativity in virtual form.

7. Population: ONE

For fans of competitive shooting games, Population: ONE is the VR equivalent of hugely popular battle royale titles. Drop into a 3D battlefield where the goal is to survive, strategize, and dominate. The ability to climb any surface, glide, and interact naturally with the environment creates fast-paced gameplay like no other.

The multiplayer encourages coordination among team members, making it rewarding for groups of friends who thrive on strategy and leadership. The game constantly evolves, introducing new maps and concepts to keep players engaged.

8. A Township Tale

If you’re looking for a group activity that leans heavily on cooperation, A Township Tale offers an open-world experience where players work together to build and manage a society. Whether it’s mining for resources, forging tools, or defending the town, players will find various roles to take on.

The game emphasizes communication and division of tasks, making it ideal for family gatherings or gaming squads. It’s one of the more relaxed multiplayer VR games but offers a sense of purpose that makes every session engaging.

9. Cook-Out: A Sandwich Tale

Nothing brings people together like food—even in VR. Cook-Out: A Sandwich Tale throws players into chaotic kitchens where the goal is to prepare food orders under increasingly frantic conditions. Collaborating with friends or family is essential as you chop, stack, and manage high-pressure cooking scenarios.

This quirky multiplayer VR game feels lighthearted and endlessly fun, with humor infused into every aspect of its gameplay. It’s easy to learn but difficult to master, making it suitable for all ages and skill levels.

10. Demeo

For board game lovers, Demeo brings tabletop RPGs into virtual reality. Gather your friends and dive into a dungeon crawling adventure packed with strategic battles and goblin-filled encounters. Each player takes on a specific class, working together to solve puzzles, defeat enemies, and uncover treasures.

The multiplayer features make it a fantastic choice for groups who enjoy cooperative games, and the rich fantasy theme keeps the energy alive. With expansion packs and updates, Demeo ensures that every session feels fresh and exciting.

Wrapping It Up

Multiplayer VR games are redefining how we connect and play, making every gathering an event to remember. From cooperative quests to competitive arenas, these games offer something for everyone, whether you’re gaming with family or catching up with friends online. The immersive nature of VR fused with the shared joy of multiplayer ensures a unique gaming experience that stands apart from traditional platforms.

Try one of these titles during your next game night, and you’ll discover why multiplayer VR is the ultimate way to bond, laugh, and enjoy together.
