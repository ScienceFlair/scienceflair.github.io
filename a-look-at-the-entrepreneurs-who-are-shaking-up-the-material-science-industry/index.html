A Look at the Entrepreneurs Who Are Shaking Up the Material Science Industry


The material science industry is rapidly evolving and this is largely thanks to a group of innovative entrepreneurs who are changing the game. With their eyes firmly set on revolutionizing the manufacturing and fabrication process, these visionaries are paving the way to a future of ground-breaking materials. Let’s take a closer look at some of these trailblazers and understand what makes their work unique, impactful, and disruptive to the industry.

1. Skylar Tibbits

The acclaimed Skylar Tibbits, a material scientist and an MIT-trained architect, is a testament to how thought-provoking minds are making the impossible, possible in material science. Tibbits founded Studio 534, a design and research lab focused on advancing the field of ‘self-assembly’, developing materials that can assemble themselves. Imagine transforming the manufacturing process by eliminating the need for assembly lines and cutting down on material waste. Tibbits’ innovation could do exactly that – making materials that saves time, reduces waste, and changes how we create and build products. One such innovation is his self-assembling furniture, which truly brings new meaning to the phrase ‘building blocks.’ You bet, Tibbits and his mind-boggling work are shaking up the material science universe.

2. Mir143

Mir143, identified initially by its Twitter account, is an anonymous entrepreneur shattering the norms of the industry. They’ve created @Mir143_lab, a lab focused on developing super-thin flexible electronics made from nanomaterials. Mir143’s work brings to reality a plethora of new applications, such as super light-weight solar panels, wraps that could change their color, and skin patches that can store medical data. Not to forget their revolutionary transparent, stretchy LED lighting! Yes, it may appear impossible at first, but Mir143 is working towards bringing it to life — their use of nanomaterials to create application after groundbreaking application is a testament to their ingenuity and ambition within the material science industry.

3. Mark Miodownik

lectrician-turned-material-scientist, Mark Miodownik, leads the pen for this forward-thinking sector. Miodownik is the director of the Institute of Making at University College London, which combines art and science to research and produce new materials. His work covers a myriad of materials, from self-healing concrete to robotics. An advocate and perhaps one of the most respected voices within the industry for emphasizing the importance of materials for design and engineering, Miodownik’s drive has led him to develop instruments that test the mechanical properties of materials. His dedication towards making strong and sustainable materials could one day support the creation of more resilient buildings, bridges, and even spaceships.

4. Nanshu Lu

Pushing the boundaries of the “Internet of Things” or IoT is Nanshu Lu, an expert in flexible electronics. Lu explores the frontiers of material science with her innovative bio-inspired hybrid materials that bond silicon to organic material. These elegant and durable materials pave the way for wearable technology. Her novel approach of developing “soft electronics”—flexible, stretchable, and manufacturable electronic materials—aims to augment human interaction with machines. From smart skin to smart textiles, her microelectronics continue to redefine tomorrow’s products, thus sparking a revolution in the material science industry.

5. Marc Duale

An expert in materials and sustainability, Marc Duale has introduced a revolutionary approach towards material science. A professor of Materials Science at Taibah University, Duale uses sustainable material innovations to catalyze the fight against climate change. His focus on the eco-friendly and waste reduction facets of materials science has led him to explore the use of waste materials for making sustainable and cheap bio-materials. Duale’s bold vision to merge sustainability with the material science field brings significant hope for a greener planet, making him a game-changer and a role model for future generations.

In Conclusion

Every entrepreneur holds a unique vision, and each has been capable of challenging existing frontiers and pushing the boundaries of material science. It is dizzying to see how material capabilities have expanded and the imageries of potential innovation that these entrepreneurs are painting. We live in exciting times, and these entrepreneurs are driving it forward. As we stand on the threshold of new materials and technologies, let us wonder and hope for a future where anything is possible, the future our fantastic entrepreneurs are writing for us.

As David Rose eloquently puts it, “Technology is such a critical element in shaping the future because it’s the reason the future is going to happen so much faster than anything we’ve ever known.” Indeed, it is the pioneering minds such as these, shaking up the material science world that will fast track us into the future of innovation.
